# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

"Private Local Transcription" (the "App") is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

## Data Collection

**This App does not collect any user data.**

- Audio, transcripts, and translations are all processed on your Mac and are never sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No user account is required
- No ads are displayed

## On-Device Processing

All features of this App are processed entirely on your device:

- **Speech recognition**: Uses Apple's speech recognition framework and WhisperKit, all on-device
- **Translation**: On-device translation via Apple's Translation framework
- **Speaker diarization**: Processed on-device
- **Summary generation**: Uses Apple's Foundation Models framework (on-device AI)

## Permissions Used

This App uses the following system permissions, all necessary for its functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Microphone | To record your voice |
| Screen Recording | To capture system audio (the other party's voice) |
| Speech Recognition | To convert speech to text |
| Calendar (read-only) | To notify you of upcoming meetings (optional) |
| Notifications | To notify you of recording status and meeting starts |

## Data Storage

Recordings and transcripts are stored only in local storage on your Mac. The default location is `~/Documents/Transcription/`, which can be changed in settings.

## Network Communication

This App uses an internet connection only for the following purpose:

- **Whisper AI model download**: Downloads speech recognition models during initial setup. After download, the App works offline

Audio data and text data are never transmitted over the network.

## Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at any particular age group and does not intentionally collect information from children under 13.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as needed. If significant changes are made, you will be notified through an app update.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please visit the [Support page](./support-en.md).
