# Support

Support page for "Private Local Transcription".

## FAQ

### What macOS version is required?

macOS 26 (Tahoe) or later is required.

### Which call apps are supported?

Since the App captures system audio at the OS level, it works with any call app — Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Discord, LINE, FaceTime, WebEx, and more.

### Is an internet connection required?

An internet connection is needed only during initial setup to download the Whisper AI model. After that, the App works completely offline.

### Is audio data sent to any server?

No. Audio, transcripts, and translations are all processed on your Mac and are never sent anywhere.

### Where are recordings stored?

By default, recordings are saved to `~/Documents/Transcription/`. You can change the location in settings.
